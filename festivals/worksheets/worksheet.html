Festivals Worksheets

Christmas Coloring Book. This is a fun activity for kids to do during the Christmas season. Students can color the pictures and then cut them out and use them to decorate their Christmas tree.

Halloween Coloring Book. This is a fun activity for kids to do during the Halloween season. Students can color the pictures and then cut them out and use them to decorate their Halloween tree.

Christmas tick the correct answer worksheet.

Listen to the clues to find a Christmas word. Then, use a dot marker or bingo chip to mark each word as it is called out. If you get five in a row, you win!
