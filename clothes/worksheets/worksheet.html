Clothes Worksheets

This worksheet has 10 pictures of winter clothing. Next to each picture are two words. One is the correct name of the item of winter clothing, and one is the wrong name. Students must look at the picture and circle the correct word.

To complete this next worksheet, students must fill in the blank space next to the pictures with the correct name of the winter clothes.

This last winter clothes worksheet is a fun bingo game. Students must cut out the pictures and arrange them in a 3×3 grid. Next, the teacher can call out one of the words and the students should try to make a line of bingo.
