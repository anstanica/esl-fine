Clothes | A Complete Lesson Plan

In this lesson plan, students will learn the names of clothes. This lesson plan is suitable for lessons teaching clothes to kids. The below lesson plan is complete with all the games, activities, and resources you need.

Warm Up

A fun way to start your clothes lesson is to listen to a clothes song. We particularly like this clothes song Put On Your Shoes by super Simple the kids love this song .
Play the song in class and ask students to try and remember the names of the clothes that they hear.
After listening, ask students what words they can remember, write them on the board, and then ask students what other clothes they know. This is a great way to activate the students’ existing knowledge of clothes and clothes vocabulary.

Show students these clothes flashcards and ask them to repeat after you.
Next, ask students to try and say each word on their own.
Once students have had enough practice, you can play a fun flashcard game to practice some more.




Rules for teaching a song for the first time:

  1. Let the kids watch the song first No interference required at this stage. So, they get an idea about the topic they are learning

  2. Teach the song using TPR , Also use an audio version Convert to audio Here. Don't let the students watch a video while you are teaching them TPR / dance, they will easily be distracted

  3. Repeat 2 OR 3 times and let the students repeat after you

  4. Perform and sing together

Practice

say the names of the farm animals out loud and ask students to repeat after you. Then, ask students to say each farm animal name on their own. A perfect approach is playing Pass & Say Or HIT and SAY Hand a flashcard to one student and ask him to say and pass to the next etc... game .

"Remember to correct their pronunciation, specially the first ones"

Games

  1. “What’s missing?“.

  2. Craft Time : print out some plain clothes and cut them out , then let the students color them

  3. Bingo : A well known game here is what you need Download the game flashcards

  4. Online Games.

Wrap up / Review

play the game ‘Simon Says’ with the expression “Put on your (hat)“. Show students how to mime putting on different items of clothing and practice several times.
